About the Conference!

Come Network at Africa's biggest Female and Youth driven Conference.

Our first event for 2022 is the Global Edition of our FLEC Conference in Nairobi, Kenya. We are so thrilled to move our “Innovation Tour” to Kenya. Join us as we host Business Owners & Amazing Leaders from Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya and the United Kingdom- Experts from across multiple sectors.
